# LinkedIn Post List — Johirul Hoq Akash / CodeMyPixel

A reusable content bank of post ideas you can rotate through. Organized by category, with hooks, templates, and a suggested cadence. Based on your audience analysis (entrepreneurs + SaaS founders + agency owners) and what actually gets engagement on your page.

---

## How to use this list

- **Pick 1 post per day** from any category below (rotate categories so your feed stays varied)
- **Aim for 5-7 posts per week** — consistency beats volume
- **Best times (founder audience):** Tue-Thu 8-10 AM or 1-3 PM (US Eastern = evening Bangladesh time)
- **Each post:** 1 hook line → 3-7 short lines → 1 CTA or question
- **Track:** save the post URL + reaction count in a sheet; double down on what works

## Engagement rules

1. **Reply to every comment within 2 hours** — boosts reach
2. **Comment on 5 other founder/agency posts daily** — reciprocity drives profile visits
3. **DM anyone who reacts 3+ times in a week** — warm lead
4. **Never post and ghost** — stay online 30 min after posting to engage
5. **Reuse top posts after 60 days** — edit the hook, repost the body
6. **Test one video post per week** — your YouTube channel (@codemypixel) is a content engine
7. **Pin your top-performing post** — first impression for profile visitors
8. **Update your headline quarterly** — keep the CTA fresh
